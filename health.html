Overview

The health care plan comparison page is an online web app based on a project ive worked on recently with Calheers
This project was a high collaboration one working across different teams to build a user interface that was easy for
people to follow when choosing the best health care option based on users health needs.

Role

Product/UI Designer

Team

50

Duration

5 month contract

Sketching Process - FigJam

Design solution - Figma

Choose health plan

This page was built to choose a health plan based on severity of health care usage

Monthly Plan details

This page shows the plan details of the chosen plan. How much the monthly plan would be as well as the yearly payment and doctor visits.

Enroll Modul

After a patient enrolls in a plan they are prompted with the question of are they sure.

Confirmation Page

This design shows that the patient is good to go and have successfully chosen a health plan that meets their needs.
